All you need is a basic understanding of business and how the Internet works. As long as you are a quick learner and are comfortable with using computers, researching on the internet, and have the know-how of social media platforms then this course is perfect for you.

Yes! Throughout the course, you'll work on real-world projects including creating social media strategies, designing content calendars, running ad campaigns, and analyzing performance metrics. You'll graduate with a portfolio of work to show potential clients or employers.
