Why become a Graphic Designer?

Think advertisements not just on television but on the bus, trains, aeroplanes, trucks or even on a chocolate bar or a candle in a box- wherever you are, there is bound to be the end product of a graphic designer who's made a design.

What skills do I need to start learning Graphic Design?

Creativity, attitude towards learning, not giving up and researching the internet to find trends … if you can guarantee the above attributes then you have the potential to become an awesome graphic designer.
